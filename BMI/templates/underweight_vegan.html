Vegan Meal Plan

Breakfast Snack Lunch Dinner
Monday Breakfast Burrito - Tortilla, 1/2 Cup Tofu scramble, 1/2 Cup Black Beans, 1/2 Cup Avocado, Spices, salsa, hot sauce for flavor 1-ounce walnuts, Apple sauce, Banana Chickpea Salad Sandwich - Whole wheat bread, 1 cup chickpeas, 1/2 cup vegan mayo, 2 tbsp mustard, Diced carrots, onion, celery for flavor, 1 cup mango on the side Pasta Marinara - 1/2 Cup lentils, 1/2 cup vegan "beef" crumbles, 1 cup edamame pasta, Olives, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, and zucchini for flavor
Tuesday Pancakes - Whole grain pancake mix with "flax egg", 2 tbsp peanut butter, 1 cup berries, maple syrup Banana Boats - 1 Banana, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 6 dates PB&J - Whole wheat bread, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 2 tbsp jam, Hemp seeds, Cinnamon "Beef" Tacos - 3 corn tortillas, 1/2 cup vegan "beef" crumbles, 1/2 cup black beans, 1 whole avocado, lettuce, tomato, vegan "sour cream"
Wednesday Avocado Toast - 1 whole avocado, 2 slices whole grain bread, sliced tomato, hemp seeds, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper Peanut Butter Toast - 2 slices whole grain bread, 3 tbsp peanut butter, 1 medium banana, cinnamon Smoothie - 1 banana, 1/2 cup strawberries, 1/4 cup walnuts, 1/4 cup chia seeds, 1/2 cup fruit juice, add water or milk of choice as needed for extra liquid, handful of spinach Pizza - 1 premade pizza crust, marinara or pizza sauce, vegan "cheese", vegan "beef crumbles" or "chicken", vegetables of choice
Thursday Oatmeal - 1 cup oats, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 1 cup soy milk, 1 medium banana, Cinnamon Vegan granola bar, 1 piece of fruit Buffalo "Chicken" Wrap - 1 tortilla, Vegan "chicken" strips, 1 tbsp buffalo sauce, Romaine lettuce, sliced tomato, 1/2 of an avocado, vegan mayo Stir-Fry - 1 cup tofu, 1 cup frozen mixed veg, Teriyaki stir-fry sauce, 1/4 cup peanuts
Friday Soy yogurt, 1 cup grapes, 1/4 cup granola, 2 tbsp almond butter, Cinnamon Trail Mix - 1/2 cup mixed nuts, 1/2 cup dried fruit, 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips Bean Burrito - 1 tortilla, 1 cup refried beans, vegan "cheese", vegetables of choice Enchiladas - 3 corn tortillas, 1/2 cup pinto beans, 1/2 cup avocado, Vegan "chicken", Green enchilada sauce
